About Me

Hello! I'm Mason Bybee, a Full-Stack Developer. Though my journey into coding is relatively recent, my background with computers is extensive and rooted in a technology-focused upbringing. My interest in computers sparked at a young age, leading me to build my own desktop computer by the age of 11. At 14, I delved into HTML and CSS, laying the groundwork for my technical skills, though I didn't fully explore their potential at the time.

My pivotal moment unfolded in early 2023. Confronted with a stagnant career path, I made a decisive shift towards Software Development. This transition reignited my passion for learning and led to a rapid expansion of my technical skillset. I've not only refreshed my knowledge of HTML and CSS, but I've also picked up a variety of programming languages and development tools including JavaScript, React, express, Python, Flask, Pygame, Terminal, and Git, among others. My ambition doesn't stop here; I am continually striving to master new languages and refine my coding practices. My ultimate goal is to channel these skills and experiences into becoming a proficient Game Developer.
